Get started

Lookback lets you record your screen, face, and audio.

In the recording panel, you’re able to select which camera or microphone to use, or disable them completely. You can also select any external displays connected to your Mac.

Open the dropdowns to have a look!

Oh, neat

You can also record any Apple device’s screen. It might be a Mac, iPhone, iPad, or AppleTV.

If you’d like to do that, please plug in a device with a Lightning USB or USB C (for AppleTV) cable into your Mac.

We support OS X 10.10+, iOS 8+, and tvOS.
